ABOUT

Believers' LoveWorld Campus Ministry is the youth arm of Believers' LoveWorld; a unique and dynamic ministry called by God to play a chief role in the end time move of the Spirit. Our Vision is to carry God's divine presence to all the people of the world, demonstrating to them the character of the Spirit. Rev Chris Oyakhilome Ph.D has a passion to see lives transformed by the power of God, and millions of young people across the globe have been affected by his ministry. Many of whom have dedicated their lives to reaching other young people in the world.

OUR MISSION

To take the divine presence of God to the people of the world and to demonstrate the character of the Holy Spirit.
The Believers' Loveworld Campus Ministry is the youth arm of the Believers' Loveworld Nation (Christ Embassy). It is a unique and dynamic ministry called by God to play a chief role in the end time move of the Spirit, with a vision to take God's divine presence to the young people in every Nation of the World; demonstrating in them, the character of the spirit.
